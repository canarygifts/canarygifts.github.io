About Us

Canary is an NFT gifting platform designed to make gifting NFTs and cryptocurrencies to non-crypto holders simple. With a unique design that allows anyone to receive NFTs or cryptocurrencies from a gift giver without going through the complicated process of setting up a wallet, Canary not only to makes gifting crypto simple but also accelerates adoption.
